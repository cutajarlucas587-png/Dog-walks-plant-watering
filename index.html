What I offer

Two things, done properly

I keep it simple so I can do it well: happy dogs and healthy plants. Pick what you need — mix and match anytime.

🐾

Dog walking & care

Dogs only — that's what I know best. Your dog gets a proper walk and fresh water, and I'll pop in to feed them if you're out for the day.

  • 30, 60 or 90-minute walks — as long as you like
  • Solo walks — your dog, my full attention
  • Feeding & drop-in visits while you're out
  • Photo update sent after every visit

🔑 Need me to let myself in? For pick-ups and feeding while you're out I may need a way in. If you leave a key, it's kept safe and labelled only with your name and house number, so there's never any mix-up.

£10–£16per walk

Plant watering

Off on holiday, or too busy in the summer heat? I'll pop round and keep your plants happy and hydrated so nothing wilts while you're away.

  • Watering front-garden beds, borders & pots
  • Hanging baskets & window boxes
  • Back-garden & indoor houseplants — if you can leave access
  • A quick photo so you know all's well

🔑 Need me inside? For back-garden or indoor houseplants I may need a way in. When you book, let me know if you'd like those done too — and if you leave a key, it's kept safe and labelled only with your name and house number, so there's never any mix-up.

£8–£14per visit
